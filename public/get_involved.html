We want the My Daddy is a Soldier Adventures community to grow and evolve. There are so many ways in which you can help us - individually, corporately, locally… We have so many dreams for this and hope you are able to join us on the journey.

Can you help us?

  • Can your organisation offer a workshop for British Army children?
  • Could you be a Garrison co-ordinator? Have you time to post flyers, put information up in your local HIVE, spread the word?
  • Could we be your charity of the year?
  • Could you run a fundraising event for My Daddy is a Soldier Adventures? It could be anything - a cake sale, 10km run, squadron event, car boot sale, dress down day at the office… we need funds to run more events for our children.
  • Could you share your talent or do you run service that could help us? We need event planners, PR experts, marketing expertise, printers, raffle prizes, experts in child behaviors, instructors, entertainers… the list is endless and we have no doubt you have a lot of what we need!
  • Are you a media outlet who can offer us a bit of publicity?

If you can help us in any way, we would be delighted to hear from you. Please email info@mydaddyisasoldieradventures.org, and one of us will get back to you soon.
