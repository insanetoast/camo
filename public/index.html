Welcome to My Daddy is a Soldier Adventures, or,
My Mummy is a Soldier too!

We're a new initiative for the little troopers at home. Our intention is to give children whose parent, or parents, are deployed, a focus.

Last year our trek of Mount Snowdon with 3 British Army children was a huge success — the support for them was simply overwhelming. You can read about it here.

We knew that many more service children could benefit from our experiences, hence My Daddy is a Soldier Adventures was born!

We're very new, and this site will grow over time. For now, have a look at our exciting events, and sign your kids up — the first is on 21st December!
